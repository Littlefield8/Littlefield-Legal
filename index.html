Meet the attorney

Dallin C. Littlefield — former prosecutor, now defense.

Dallin is an experienced criminal defense attorney with extensive experience on both sides of the courtroom. Having served as a prosecutor for various government entities — including several Utah municipalities and the Utah Attorney General's Office — he brings a unique perspective to his defense practice. That prosecutorial background makes him an effective advocate in negotiations, helping to secure favorable outcomes for his clients.

He earned a degree in Psychology from Brigham Young University in Provo, then his Juris Doctor from Gonzaga University School of Law. His background also includes service as a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Ghana, West Africa, along with experience in retail pharmaceuticals, real estate, legislative processes, and municipal government.
