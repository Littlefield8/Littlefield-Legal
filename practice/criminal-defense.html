Plea in Abeyance — a Utah tool worth understanding

Utah's plea-in-abeyance statute can, in the right case, let a charge be dismissed entirely after a period of compliance. It isn't available for every charge — DUI, for instance, is specifically excluded by statute — but where it applies, it's underused and worth understanding before you sign anything.
